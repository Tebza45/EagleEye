I'm Lihle Tshabe

Photographer | Graphic Designer

-ABOUT ME- A passionate photographer and self-taught designer with a deep love for capturing life’s most meaningful moments. Photography was never part of the plan—but passion has a way of finding you. As graduate from the University of Johannesburg, I was set on a different path until I picked up a camera in 2021. What started as a simple hobby quickly became an obsession, and before I knew it, I had built a thriving photography business. I specialize in graduations, weddings, maternity, and corporate events, blending creativity with precision to tell powerful stories through my lens. My mission is to capture authentic emotions, timeless beauty, and unforgettable moments that you can cherish forever. Every picture has a story—let’s create yours together!
